NYC Maps 
- Most trips originate and end on Manhattan island and in the approximate center of the service area.

Weekday Detail 
- On weekdays, rush hour (7-9 am and 4-6 pm) had the highest volume of trips, while peak time on the weekends is closer to noon.

- Thursdays have the highest trip volume, closely followed by Wednesdays.

Weather Detail 
- On weeks with spikes in precipitation, trips decrease.

- As the temperature drops towards the end of 2023, trips begin to drop off.
